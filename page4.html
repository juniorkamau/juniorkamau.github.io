CUSTOMER SERVICE CHARTER

The customer service charter confirms publicly the roles and responsibilities of VIWASCO Ltd and the rights & responsibilities of our customers.

LEARN MORE

MISSION.


To ensure provision of efficient and effective quality water services in our area of jurisdiction and beyond.
Core Values 

VISION.


To be the leading Water Service Provider in Kenya providing effective and efficient service that meets the expectations of customers and stakeholders. 

Aims of the Charter .


The charter aims at making the functions of the company transparent by:-
 Creating customer awareness
 Advertising the company
 Protecting the image of the company

CORE VALUES.

To achieve our Vision and Mission, VIWASCO Ltd operates under the following guiding principles, philosophies, firm’s ethos and corporate values:-
 Professionalism
 Teamwork
 Responsiveness
 Customer-focus
 Reliability
 

OUR COMMITMENTS

To ensure that our customers have appropriate services, VIWASCO Ltd commits to do the following:-
 Enforce water quality monitoring
 Undertake planning, infrastructure development and update maintenance procedures in order to minimize interruptions to water supply.
 Have accurate and timely billing with bills issued monthly.
 Demand that all our staff and agents have a customer focus in all their activities and be responsible to customer needs.

Our Responsibilities

Our responsibilities include but not limited to the following:
 Improve operational efficiency and deliver well functional infrastructure
 Ensure financial and operational sustainability
 Expand coverage with emphasis on improving access to water services in informal settlements
 Contribute to poverty alleviation, achieving gender equity, educating communities in good health and hygiene practices, promote awareness on HIV/AIDS and protect the environment.
 Capacity building of our water agents to ensure that they embrace efficiency, accountability and responsibility in service delivery
 Monitor and evaluate performance against targets for the company and staff.

Customers Responsibilities

To ensure that we serve you better, we request our customers to:
 Provide us with a current and functional mobile number for our quick response/dissemination of information
 Allow authorized VIWASCO Ltd staff access to all meters at all times for the purpose of reading and testing.
 Use water responsibly
 Report bursts or leaks to the office or any staff immediately you notice
 Report illegal water connections or reconnections
 Pay your water bills promptly and ensure your connection number is clearly indicated in all forms of payments
 Do not offer gifts, money or other favors to VIWASCO Ltd staff members
 Demand a receipt for any payment made.
